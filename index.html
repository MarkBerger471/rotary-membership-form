What Happens Next?

1

Board Review

3-4 weeks

The Club Board will review your application and submit it to all members of the club for review. This process takes 3-4 weeks by definition.

2

Invoice from Treasurer

Upon approval, the Club Treasurer will send you an invoice covering both the one-time registration fee and the annual membership fee.

3

Payment & Confirmation

Pay the invoice and submit your proof of payment. You will then receive your welcome materials and official membership confirmation.

4

Induction Ceremony

The Club President will coordinate with you the date for the New Member Induction Ceremony — a special event during a club meeting where you will be officially introduced and celebrated as our newest member.

Classification Talk

Our Club Meeting Organizer will schedule a club meeting exclusively for your personal introduction to all club members — the so-called Classification Talk, where you present yourself and your professional background.
